Support and Resistance in Crypto Explained

Support and resistance are foundational concepts in cryptocurrency trading and help identify key price levels.

What is Support?

Support is a level where demand may prevent price from falling further.

What is Resistance?

Resistance is a level where selling pressure may prevent price from rising.

Why These Levels Matter

  • Identify entry points
  • Plan exits
  • Understand market structure

Final Thoughts

Consistent analysis of key levels helps traders make more structured decisions.
